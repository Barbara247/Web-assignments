Preparation Methods

1. How to Prepare Jollof Rice

First, blend tomatoes, onions, and pepper into a smooth paste. Heat oil in a pan and fry the mixture for a few minutes. Add spices like curry, thyme, and salt. Pour in water and add the washed rice. Cover and allow it to cook slowly until the rice absorbs the stew and becomes soft and tasty.

2. How to Prepare Banku

Mix corn dough and cassava dough together with water until smooth. Pour the mixture into a pot and place it on fire. Stir continuously to prevent lumps. Add salt to taste. Keep stirring until it thickens and becomes smooth and stretchy. Serve hot with pepper sauce or soup.
